# Lead-Quality-Analysis-Marketing-Performance-Dashboard
The project focused on analyzing lead data across multiple dimensions such as ad creatives, publisher sources, customer attributes, and verification scores to uncover patterns affecting lead quality and conversion rates.

<b>Dasboard</b>
<b> Key Features:</b>

✔ Executive KPI dashboard showing Total Leads, Lead Quality %, Closed Rate %, and Bad Lead %
✔ Trend analysis of lead quality over time to identify performance improvements or declines
✔ Lead volume vs quality comparison to evaluate trade-offs in scaling campaigns
✔ Ad creative (widget) performance analysis to identify high-performing designs
✔ Publisher-level comparison to assess source quality differences
✔ Address & phone verification impact on lead quality
✔ Debt level vs lead quality analysis using scatter plots

<b>Tools Used:</b>
Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
SQL (Data extraction & transformation)
Excel (Data preprocessing)

 <b>Outcome:</b>

The dashboard provided actionable insights into improving lead quality by optimizing ad creatives, prioritizing high-performing publisher campaigns, and enforcing better data verification standards.

These insights help businesses balance lead volume with quality, improve conversion rates, and maximize return on marketing spend.
